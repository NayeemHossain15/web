About Us

The Department of Computer Science & Engineering is committed to advancing the frontiers of computing through cutting-edge research, innovative teaching, and impactful community engagement. Our mission is to prepare students for successful careers in technology and contribute to solving global challenges through interdisciplinary collaboration and innovation.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission

To empower students with the knowledge, skills, and ethical foundation to innovate and lead in the field of computer science, fostering solutions to global challenges.

Vision

To be a global leader in computer science education and research, driving technological advancements and societal impact.

History of the Department

Established in 1980, the Department of Computer Science & Engineering has grown from a small program to a leading center for technological innovation. Over the decades, we have produced thousands of graduates who have made significant contributions to academia, industry, and society. Key milestones include the launch of our Ph.D. program in 1995 and the establishment of our AI Research Lab in 2010.
