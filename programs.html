Academic Programs

Our academic programs are designed to equip students with the skills and knowledge needed to excel in the rapidly evolving field of computer science and engineering. From undergraduate to doctoral levels, we offer rigorous curricula and hands-on opportunities.

Our Programs

Bachelor of Science in Computer Science & Engineering

A comprehensive undergraduate program focusing on core CS concepts and practical skills.

Master of Science in Computer Science

Advanced training in specialized areas with opportunities for research and industry projects.

Ph.D. in Computer Science

A research-intensive program for those pursuing cutting-edge contributions to the field.

Undergraduate Program Details

BSc in Computer Science & Engineering

Our BSc program spans four years and covers foundational topics like algorithms, data structures, and software engineering, alongside electives in AI, cybersecurity, and more.

  • Duration: 4 years
  • Credits: 120
  • Key Courses: Programming, Databases, Operating Systems

Graduate Program Details

MSc in Computer Science

A 2-year program offering advanced coursework and research opportunities in areas like machine learning and cloud computing.

  • Duration: 2 years
  • Credits: 36
  • Options: Thesis or Non-Thesis

Ph.D. in Computer Science

A 3-5 year program focused on original research, guided by world-class faculty.

  • Duration: 3-5 years
  • Focus: Original Research
